"Rejoice in hope, endure in affliction, persevere in prayer." – Romans 12:12

This is the official main logo of WYC 2025: Anchored in Christ. Inspired by the theme, the anchor serves as a powerful symbol of Christ - our firm foundation and guide amid the shifting tides of life.

Anchor : It is a symbol for firm hope - the hope that we have in Christ. The shape of the anchor simulates the shape of the Cross. In the Catholic Church, it is used by the early Christians as the powerful symbol of Christ - our firm foundation and guide amid the shifting tides of life.

Waves : Representing the various circumstances and challenges we encounter along our journey.

Rejoice. Endure. Persevere : We are grounded on our theme verse as a community, Romans 12:12 “Rejoice in hope, endure in affliction, persevere in prayer.”

Anchored in Christ : Despite the ever-changing nature of the waves and storms, we will not be shaken because Christ is our anchor. He is our unfailing hope. We are Anchored in Christ.

The MFC Youth Awards are the highest recognition in the MFC Youth Section. These are presented to coordinators, young men and women, and particular schools/ campuses/ chapters, for their exceptional leadership and service to the community and the Church.

The ark of Champions

As pilgrims of hope, we are never alone on our life’s journey. We walk with God, our families, and our brothers and sisters in the community. This year’s THE ARK OF CHAMPIONS is an opportunity to provide the youth with an immersive and close contact environment with their fellow youth, even outside their areas.

Just like Noah, who trusted God and built an ark to survive the great flood, we are called to trust God as we face the waves and storms of life. The Ark reminds us that even when the waters rise, we stay anchored in Christ. Protecting and defending the Ark means protecting the faith, hope, and mission God has given us, sailing together as one crew and community of believers.

Frequently Asked Questions

World Youth Congress is an annual gathering that brings together youth servant leaders and couple coordinators from our community across the Philippines and other international countries, with an expected attendance of approximately 1,500 participants.

The WYC 2025 will be held in Nueva Ecija Coliseum, Palayan City on May 30 to June 1, 2025.

Register at bit.ly/2025WYC. Contact your areas for the delegation details.

You can register online via the official website or through your local church coordinators.

How to get there?

From Cubao To Nueva Ecija Coliseum

Estimated time: 3 to 4 hours

Estimated fare: 300 to 400 PHP

    Ride a bus to Cabanatuan City. You have two options: Genesis Transport, which departs every 2 hours, or Five Star, which departs every 5 hours.
    From the Cabanatuan City Terminal, take a jeepney to the Palayan City.
    Alight at Palayan City National High School and walk towards the venue.
    Ensure you arrive at least 30 minutes before your chosen departure time to allow for ticketing and boarding procedures.​
